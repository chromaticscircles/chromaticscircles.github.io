About Chromatic Circles

Chromatic Circles is a mindful art space dedicated to mandala, dot mandala, and pattern-based artworks. Each piece is created slowly and intentionally, reflecting balance, harmony, and inner stillness through geometric expression.

About the Artist

Deepti Padmanabha

I started my mandala journey during the lockdown period of the COVID-19 pandemic, purely out of curiosity and the thought of learning a new art. With more time on my hands at home, I decided to explore mandalas as a hobby.

I began with simple designs, gradually understanding the importance of patterns and symmetry required to create balance. Often, I would start a piece around 11:00 pm and continue working through the night until 3:00 am, fully immersed in the design taking shape.

As my passion grew, I started exploring dot mandalas and later expanded my practice to include Lippan art. Sleepless nights, complete focus on a single artwork, and the appreciation I received for my work kept motivating me and shaped what my art has become today.

Over time, this slow and mindful learning transformed into a deeply fulfilling creative journey. Along the way, I sold some of my artworks and donated the proceeds to Rotary RajMahal Vilas.

At present, I am working on a round canvas measuring approximately 23 inches â€” something that once felt impossible but has now become a reality created with my whole heart.

Reflecting on this journey, I see how curiosity gradually turned into confidence, and a simple interest evolved into a meaningful form of artistic expression that continues to keep me engaged and inspired.
