About the Artist

Chromatic Circles is a mindful art practice rooted in patience, symmetry, and quiet focus. Each artwork is created slowly, allowing the process itself to become a form of meditation. What began as curiosity gradually evolved into a dedicated exploration of mandala, dot mandala, and lippan art.
