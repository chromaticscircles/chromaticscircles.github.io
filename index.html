About Chromatic Circles

Chromatic Circles is a mindful art space dedicated to mandala, dot mandala, and pattern-based artworks. Rooted in symmetry, colour, and rhythm, each piece is created slowly and intentionally, reflecting balance, harmony, and inner stillness through detailed geometric expression.

About the Artist

My mandala journey began during the COVID-19 lockdown, sparked by curiosity and the desire to learn a new art form. What started as a hobby soon evolved into a deep passion. Through sleepless nights, layered patterns, and mindful repetition, I explored mandala, dot mandala, and lippan art as a form of meditation and expression.
